3D PRINTED HOUSING

Technology emerging worldwide, using local materials.

Affordable 3D printed housing is an up-and-coming technology that's better for everyone. When made with local and natural materials, these houses can be way better for the environment than a "normal" house. While they may not be as durable as your home, they are supposedly hurricane and earthquake resistant. 3D printing is not new technology, but it's being reinvented and made better all the time. Projects like this are giving people safe homes, good jobs, and a new mindset on 3D printing.
