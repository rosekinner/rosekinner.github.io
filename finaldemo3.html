The very first scale model for 3D printing was in 1984, invented by Charles Hull. The first "house-sized" 3D printer came out in 2006. In 2016, in China, a revolutionary concrete mansion was printed. We've come a long way from making random sculptures in the library. We now have 3D printed neighborhoods, two-story buildings, and affordable homes. 3D printing has a long way to go, but the progress being made right now is astonishing.
